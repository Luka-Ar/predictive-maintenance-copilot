# Machine Operating Manual

Normal operating conditions:
- Air temperature: 295K to 310K
- Process temperature: 305K to 325K
- Rotational speed: 1000 to 1600 rpm
- Torque: 20 to 55 Nm
- Tool wear: below 150 minutes

High torque may indicate excessive mechanical load, friction, misalignment, worn bearings, or tool degradation.

A large temperature difference between air temperature and process temperature may indicate cooling inefficiency, abnormal heat generation, or process instability.

If torque exceeds 60 Nm, inspect load conditions, lubrication, shaft alignment, and tool condition.
